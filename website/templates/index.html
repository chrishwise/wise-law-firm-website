Firm Overview

At Wise Law Firm PLC, we believe that attentive, well-prepared representation is key to resolving disputes efficiently. Located in Fairfax, we have served clients throughout Virginia and across the nation since 1989, and we are proud to bring 100 combined years of experience in business law and construction law to protecting your company's interests.

Our attorneys have litigated extensively before state and federal courts and have the industry knowledge and legal background to effectively prosecute or defend cases arising from contract disputes and related construction issues. In addition, we represent people seeking compensation for injuries sustained in an auto accident or other incident. No matter how complex your matter is, we maintain our commitment to helping you fulfill your objectives.
